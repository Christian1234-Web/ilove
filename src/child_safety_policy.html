Capatuno - Child Safety and Anti-Exploitation Policy (CSAE)

Last Updated: August 29, 2025
Effective Date: August 29, 2025
Policy Version: 1.0

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

Capatuno is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. We have zero tolerance for content, conduct, or interactions that exploit, harm, or endanger children in any way.

2. Prohibited Content and Conduct

2.1 Strictly Prohibited

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
  • Content that sexualizes, grooms, abuses, or otherwise exploits children
  • Content that promotes, encourages, or instructs child abuse or exploitation
  • Attempts to engage minors in sexual or romantic conversations
  • Sharing, requesting, or soliciting intimate images of minors
  • Financial exploitation or coercion of minors
  • Facilitating offline meetings between adults and minors through our platform

2.2 Age-Inappropriate Content

  • Sexually explicit content accessible to minors
  • Content promoting illegal activities to minors
  • Content that encourages self-harm or dangerous behavior by minors
  • Predatory behavior targeting minors

3. Age Verification and Protection Measures

3.1 Minimum Age Requirements

  • Users must be 13 years or older to create an account
  • Users under 18 require parental consent where legally required
  • Users under 16 have additional privacy protections under applicable laws

3.2 Account Verification

  • All new accounts undergo automated screening
  • Suspicious account patterns trigger manual review
  • Photo verification may be required for financial features
  • Regular re-verification for accounts engaging in financial transactions

4. Detection and Response Systems

4.1 Automated Detection

  • PhotoDNA Technology: Microsoft PhotoDNA integration for CSAM detection
  • Machine Learning: AI-powered content analysis for harmful material
  • Behavioral Analysis: Pattern recognition for predatory behavior
  • Communication Monitoring: Real-time message scanning for grooming attempts

4.2 Human Review

  • Trained content moderation team
  • 24/7 escalation procedures for urgent cases
  • Regular training on child safety best practices
  • Clear escalation paths for suspected criminal activity

5. Response and Enforcement

5.1 Immediate Actions

  • Account Suspension: Immediate suspension pending investigation
  • Content Removal: Automatic removal of flagged content
  • Evidence Preservation: Secure preservation of evidence for authorities
  • User Protection: Immediate protection measures for potential victims

5.2 Legal Reporting

  • Mandatory reporting to National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Cooperation with local law enforcement agencies
  • International cooperation through appropriate channels
  • Compliance with all applicable reporting requirements

6. Financial Transaction Protections

6.1 Minor Protection in Financial Features

  • Enhanced verification required for users under 18
  • Transaction limits for minor accounts
  • Parental oversight for financial activities
  • Prohibition of financial exploitation schemes targeting minors

7. Technology and Infrastructure

7.1 Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption with law enforcement access provisions
  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Advanced threat detection systems

7.2 Data Protection

  • Minimal data collection from minors
  • Enhanced privacy protections for users under 18
  • Secure deletion of data from banned accounts
  • Compliance with COPPA and GDPR-K requirements

  • Direct law enforcement contact procedures
  • Immediate platform safety measures
  • Crisis communication protocols

8.1 Regular Reporting

  • Annual transparency reports on child safety efforts
  • Statistics on detection, reporting, and enforcement actions
  • Policy effectiveness assessments
  • Continuous improvement commitments

8.2 External Audits

  • Regular third-party audits of child safety measures
  • Independent assessment of policy effectiveness
  • Public reporting of audit results
  • Implementation of recommended improvements

9. International Compliance

9.1 Legal Compliance

  • Compliance with local laws in all operating jurisdictions
  • Adherence to international child protection standards
  • Regular legal review of policies and procedures
  • Proactive compliance with emerging regulations

This policy is reviewed and updated regularly to address emerging threats and incorporate industry best practices. Users will be notified of material changes through in-app notifications and email communications.
